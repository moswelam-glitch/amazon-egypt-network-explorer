FC — Inbound Operations

Dock → Receive → Stow. Each item progresses through defined system states before becoming available for picking.

1 DOCK

Entry point. Associate opens Dock Master: Scheduled → Arrived → Closed.

Gate Check-In: Truck verified for timeliness. Yard: TDR set before unloading. Unloading: Scan every item — accepts shortages, NOT excess. Confirmation: Signed document → shipment to NYR.

2 RECEIVE

Induct into inventory. Receive type: Each | Prep | Case | Pallet.

Scanning: Validates dimensions/weight/specs. Expiry: Checked; missing → Problem Solve. Trolley: Status → NYS (Not Yet Stowed).

3 STOW

Physical placement into storage. System recommends location by height/weight.

Rules: BOAATS • ASIN limits/bin. Reactive Stowing: Priority for SPX5 transshipments. Done: Status → Available.

FC — ICQA (Inventory Control & Quality Assurance)

Ensures inventory accuracy through systematic counts and audits. Direct Processes (counting) and Indirect Processes (research & investigation).

Direct Processes (Counting)

📋 Simple Bin Count (SBC)

Verifies total unit quantity in a bin matches system record.

AspectDetails
Assignment~90% system-assigned
CapabilityCannot adjust inventory — only flags discrepancies
EscalationTriggers Cycle Count if recount still mismatches
Hour TargetShould be ≥50% of total ICQA hours
PermissionBeginner
🔄 Cycle Count (CC)

Scans each item individually (barcode hierarchy) with 6-sided check to verify and correct at ASIN level.

AspectDetails
CapabilityCan add/delete inventory records
ValidationRequires two counters to agree before adjustment
TriggerMainly from defective (mismatched) SBCs
PermissionIntermediate / Expert
🎯 Simple Record Count (SRC)

Counts only a specific ASIN in a bin — targeted and precise.

AspectDetails
Generation100% system-generated (SIPS or Amnesty triggers)
ValidationTwo consecutive matching counts to adjust
ExpiryExpires after 10 days if not completed
PermissionIntermediate
🧹 Amnesty

Recovers misplaced items (floors, stations, conveyors), verifies, stows back via Amnesty Addback Tool.

AspectDetails
Sort CategoriesStow, Damage, Remove, Prep, Problem Solve, Expiry
DownstreamTriggers SRCs to find source bins
TrackingMonitored via APMT metric
📅 Expiry / ERDR

Verifies expiration dates on shelf-life items (Grocery, Cosmetics/Drugstore).

AspectDetails
RuleEarliest date entered if multiple units
Missing DatesItems without dates → Pending Research
ImportanceCritical health & safety compliance
🔒 IRDR (Weekly Compliance)

Random weekly cycle counts measuring overall accuracy. SOX-required.

AspectDetails
ComplianceSOX-required — strict regulatory mandate
Bin SelectionSecret randomized list — undisclosed to counters
Formula(Shortages + Overages) / Records × 1,000,000
CountersExpert-only with SOX training certification
Why it matters: Ultimate measure of FC inventory health. Results reported to finance/compliance. Failures trigger mandatory root cause investigations.
